ABOUT THE SUPERVISOR

A Bowl Born from
Ginza Sushi Mastery

MAGURO & SHARI is a tuna-bowl specialty restaurant supervised by Hiroyuki Sato, master chef of Hakkoku — one of Ginza's most sought-after sushi restaurants.

Driven by the wish to bring truly delicious food to more people, he distilled the essence of sushi — wild bluefin tuna and red-vinegar rice — into the casual form of a donburi bowl.

Since opening in Shibuya in October 2021, we have welcomed a steady stream of guests from around the world and the neighborhood alike.

Hiroyuki Sato
Master Chef of Hakkoku, Ginza
★ MICHELIN-STARRED CHEF, 4 YEARS RUNNING

"Sushi is a tool for making people happy. By serving truly delicious food in the casual style of a rice bowl, I hope MAGURO & SHARI brings one more smile into the world — nothing would make me happier."

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Authentic Flavor,
Within Everyone's Reach.

Wild bluefin tuna, raised on fresh prey in cold open seas, carries a depth of fat and umami that farmed tuna simply cannot match. We pair it with authentic red-vinegar shari — a sushi master's craft and philosophy, served in a single bowl.

— FROM THE OWNER OF MAGURO & SHARI

PREMIUM CUT
米
Koshiibuki Rice from Niigata

We use Niigata-grown Koshiibuki, a variety rarely found outside the prefecture. Its firm grains take on red vinegar beautifully, creating shari with real substance.

酢
Original Red-Vinegar Blend

A house blend of red vinegar perfected through endless trial and error. Its depth and umami draw out the sweetness of the tuna's fat.

技
Finished with Nikiri Soy

Each slice of tuna is briefly dipped in nikiri soy before being laid over the rice — the sushi master's craft, alive in every bowl.

OUR SHARI

Shari Completes
the Tuna

The other star of MAGURO & SHARI is our red-vinegar shari.

Niigata Koshiibuki rice meets an original red-vinegar blend supervised by Hakkoku, creating shari that is rich, deep, and mellow.

Not plain rice, not white vinegar — the pairing of red-vinegar shari with bluefin tuna is the very heart of our name: MAGURO & SHARI.
